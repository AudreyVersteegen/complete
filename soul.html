The Power of Music


The right song can bring a smile to a tear stained face, life into a 99-year-old’s eyes, memories back into the mind of a patient suffering from dementia, and inspiration to all who are willing to listen. Music is such a staple in our lives that we do not always notice it within all the forms it exists. Music is in the rhythm of a fawn prancing on a field, the tempo of a beating heart, and the sound of an autumn leaf crunching under one’s foot. Music is in the nature that surrounds and lives within us.
